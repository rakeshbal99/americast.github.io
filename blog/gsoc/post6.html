Google Summer of Code 2018

Week 9:12

Sunday, 12th August 2018.

This month, I attened JuliaCon held at University College London, London, United Kingdom. I got the opportunity to interact with the Julia community and got exposure to the latest developments going on with Julia at the core. I presented a poster regarding my work and it was appreciated by many at the poster presentation session. Along with that, the mentors of other GSoC students pointed out methods of improvement on which I could work on. I witnessed the release of Julia v1.0 which was a great achievement for the community which I had already become a part of.

I also spent time reading research materials on Sensitivity Analysis during this period on which I was to work in the upcoming months, with the community. I went through a thesis from UCL which discussed on Sensitivity Analysis in Systems Biology Modelling.

I enjoyed my time working with Julia and I look forward to keep doing so for the time being.
